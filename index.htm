How I Work

A simple process that keeps users supported, tools organized, and communication clear.

Listen & Understand

I start by learning how your team works, what tools you use, and where people feel stuck. This can be a short call or written notes.

Result: Clear list of tools, pain points, and support needs.

Map Tools & Flows

Next, I map out how tickets, messages, and tasks move across your tools. This helps us see what can be simplified or better documented.

Result: Simple view of your support process and key touchpoints.

Set Up & Clean Up

I help tidy up folders, contacts, and workflows, and set up small changes like tags, templates, or basic automations that make support work smoother.

Result: Easier navigation, cleaner data, and clearer steps for your team.

Document & Guide

I create short guides, checklists, or simple videos so people know exactly what to do when a request comes in or a tool misbehaves.

Result: Reusable help for your team and faster answers for users.

Review & Improve

After things are live, we review what worked, what still feels heavy, and what can be improved. Small, steady changes keep support strong.

Result: Ongoing improvements without chaos or confusion.

About Joanna

I am Joanna Sylvester, focused on IT support, CRM, and customer-facing work. I am currently transitioning into tech with clear direction: help teams keep their tools stable and make sure customers feel supported and informed.

My learning path includes structured IT support classes, self-paced study, and practice projects where I use tools like Google Drive, Dropbox, Slack, Loom, and Microsoft Teams. I enjoy breaking problems into small parts, checking the basics, and writing notes that someone else can easily follow.

I see IT support as continuous support. It is not only about fixing the issue once. It is about building a simple, repeatable way for people to get help without feeling lost. That is why I care about documentation, checklists, and how the message is delivered, not only the final answer.

What you can expect from me:

  • I ask clear questions before I start, so we solve the right problem.
  • I write notes and guides that non-technical people can understand.
  • I respect people’s time by keeping communication short, warm, and direct.
  • I care about privacy and data safety when dealing with customer and company information.
  • I treat every task, big or small, as a chance to build trust.

If you need someone who is growing in IT but already serious about process, clarity, and customer care, I would be glad to support your team.
